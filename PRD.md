# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
# Manacles Solutions Corporate Website

---

# 1. Project Overview

## Project Name
Manacles Solutions Website

## Project Type
Premium Corporate Website

## Industry
Global Sourcing, Procurement, Logistics, Supply Chain & Distribution

## Objective
Build a world-class corporate website for Manacles Solutions that positions the company as a trusted international sourcing, procurement, logistics, and supply-chain partner.

The website should communicate:
- Global operational scale
- International logistics capabilities
- Enterprise-level professionalism
- Trust and reliability
- Industry expertise
- Modern procurement and sourcing solutions

The final product must feel comparable to:
- DP World
- Maersk
- Flexport
- DHL Global Forwarding
- C.H. Robinson

The website must feel cinematic, premium, modern, responsive, and enterprise-grade.
